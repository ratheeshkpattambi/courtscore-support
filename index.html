CourtScore

Giant badminton scoreboard and scorekeeper for iPhone and iPad. Tap or swipe up to score and swipe down to fix a mistake. Apple Watch scoring is optional.

Free · Offline · Private — No accounts, ads, analytics, tracking, or data collection.

How it works

  1. Open CourtScore at the court.
  2. Tap the left or right side to add a point.
  3. Swipe up to add a point or swipe down to remove one.
  4. Optionally use the paired Apple Watch app to control the same score from your wrist.
  5. Tap the gear icon for next game, reset, and rules.
