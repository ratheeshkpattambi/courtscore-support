CourtScore

Giant badminton scoreboard for iPhone. Tap left or right to score. Hold to fix a mistake. Readable from across the court.

Free · Offline · Private — No accounts, ads, analytics, tracking, or data collection.

How it works

  1. Open CourtScore at the court.
  2. Tap the left or right side to add a point.
  3. Hold a side to remove a point if you tapped by mistake.
  4. Tap the gear icon for next game, reset, and rules.
