CourtScore

Support

CourtScore is a free giant badminton scoreboard for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

How do I use the app?

  1. Open CourtScore at the court — scoring starts immediately.
  2. Tap the left side to add a point for Player A.
  3. Tap the right side to add a point for Player B.
  4. Swipe up to add a point or swipe down to remove one.
  5. Tap the gear icon (top right) for next game, reset, and rules.

Which iPhones are supported?

Any iPhone running iOS 16 or later, including iPhone SE, mini, and Pro Max models. CourtScore uses a landscape-only full-screen scoreboard on iPhone and iPad.

How does Apple Watch synchronization work?

Install CourtScore on a paired Apple Watch running watchOS 10 or later and open the app on both devices. Score changes work in both directions. If the devices temporarily disconnect, the newest score is exchanged when they reconnect.

Does the app need an account or internet connection?

No. The app has no login and works fully offline.

What data does the app collect?

None. The current score may pass directly between your paired iPhone and Apple Watch. It is not sent to the developer or any third party.
