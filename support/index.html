CourtScore

Support

CourtScore is a free on-device badminton scorekeeper for iPhone and iPad.

How do I use the app?

  1. Open CourtScore at the court.
  2. Tap a player's side to add one point.
  3. Use Undo if you entered a point by mistake.
  4. Tap Next Game when a game ends.
  5. Use Reset Match to start over.

Does the app need an account or internet connection?

No. The app has no login and is designed to work without a network connection.

What data does the app collect?

None. Processing stays on the device.
