Information We Collect

CourtScore does not collect personal information.

Match scores exist only in memory while the app is open. If the Apple Watch app is installed, the current score can pass directly between the paired iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. Nothing is transmitted to the developer, cloud storage, advertisers, analytics providers, or other third parties.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The app does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, user accounts, or third-party data-collection SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
